CEYLON CULTURE

CULTURE BY SRILANKA

Embracing the artistry of gastronomy, Hotel Marriott is dedicated to crafting exceptional dishes that tell a story of freshness and locality. Our culinary philosophy revolves around sourcing the finest ingredients from local markets, ensuring a connection to the community and a commitment to sustainability. Whether nestled in the serene hills or along the picturesque coast, our meals are a testament to the dedication of local farmers and fishermen. From the lush fields without preservatives to the morning catch brought in by skilled fishermen, each plate at Culture By Ceylon -Marriott Restaurant narrates an evocative culinary journey, inviting guests to savor the essence of locally procured, wholesome ingredients.
