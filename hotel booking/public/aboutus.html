About us

GET BETTER EXPERIENCE WITH US

Experience a seamless blend of traditional Sri Lankan charm and modern luxury at the Marriot hotel. Meticulously designed rooms and suites offer a retreat where comfort meets sophistication, ensuring a peaceful night's rest after exploring Kandy's vibrant surroundings. Wake up to breathtaking panoramic views surrounded by lush greenery and misty hills. Embark on a culinary journey with masterfully crafted dishes, from authentic Sri Lankan delicacies to international flavors. Indulge in wellness at our spa and wellness center, offering traditional Ayurvedic treatments and a refreshing pool. Immerse yourself in Kandy's rich culture with curated experiences to nearby landmarks, guided by our concierge. Our warm hospitality creates a genuine sense of home, while our event spaces cater to romantic getaways, family vacations, and special celebrations. Committed to sustainability, we take pride in eco-friendly amenities and responsible sourcing, ensuring you enjoy a guilt-free stay at our environmentally conscious haven.
