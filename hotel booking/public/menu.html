MAIN COURSES

Breakfast

Traditional Sri Lankan Rice and Curry
A flavorsome combination of rice paired with an assortment of curries, including dhal curry, coconut sambol, and fish or chicken curry.
String Hoppers with Kiri Hodi
Soft, steamed rice noodle patties served with a fragrant coconut milk gravy, creating a unique and delicious dish.
Hoppers with Egg or Honey Kiri Hodi
Crispy-edged hoppers, a bowl-shaped pancake made from fermented rice flour batter, served with a choice of eggs or a sweet coconut milk gravy.
Kiribath with Lunu Miris

Roti with Katta Sambol

Tea or Coffee with Jaggery

Lunch

Rice & Curry
